It was a disastrous month for Moiva in terms of Google Search performance. The coverage dropped from 1.5K to 50 pages.

As a consequence, Google doesnâ€™t suggest Moiva.io in search results when people look for software comparison.

I believe it was due to Moivaâ€™s emphasis on graphical data representation (rather than textual). Despite the fact that all of Moivaâ€™s charts are accompanied by accessibility information, Google appears to have difficulty parsing and understanding graphical data. As a consequence, all the pages look the same to Google.

Another factor that may have contributed to the issue is the change of the structure of Moivaâ€™s URLs which I made at the start of the year: the compare parameter was replaced with a combination of npm and github parameters. I had two options to avoid penalties from Google:
